How It Works

Create and auction your stamps

  • Set up your wallet

    Once you’ve set up your wallet of choice, connect it to StampAuction by clicking the marketplace in the top right corner. Learn about the wallets we support.

  • Create your auction

    Click Create and set up your auction. Add social links, a description, and set a reserve price for your rare stamps.

  • Add your stamps

    Upload high-resolution images of your stamps, add a title and description, and customize your auction with bidding terms.

  • List them for sale

    Choose between auctions, fixed-price listings, and declining-price listings. You choose how you want to sell your stamps!
